B

Timeline Heatmap

A visual timeline where each row is a departure time and columns show hours into the passage. Color-coded by conditions (green=good sailing, yellow=light wind, red=heavy weather). Gives a feel for what you'll encounter.

C

Table + Best Route on Map

Ranked table (like A) but automatically shows the top-ranked route on the map. Click any row to switch the displayed route. Combines data scanning with spatial context.
